Martin Luther King Jr.

Early Life

Martin Luther king was born on January 15th 1929 in Atlanta,Georgia U.S. As a child he was brought up in a very religious home with his two siblings. During his teen life he witnessed firsthand the amount of segregation that divided his comunity. Additionally he was know for his ability of public-speaking. This was what began his career of advocating for the rights of others.

Activism and Leadership

Before King has began leading the movement Rosa Parks and Claudette Colvin had alrady stood up the discrimination in buses but it wasnt until King in 1955 decided enough was enough. Planned and led by him the bus boycott began it lasted 385 days and the situation was so intense his house was bombed. it wasnt until the law was changed that they stopped. This tranformed in him into a national figure. As well as this he also organized non-violent protests against southern segregation laws. By leading marches, protests and sit-ins for voting and right changed happened.All throughout the 1960s it seemed racism became a thing of the past.
